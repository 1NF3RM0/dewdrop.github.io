About DEW Drop Construction

DEW Drop Construction Company is a leading construction service provider based in Marondera, Zimbabwe. We specialize in painting, plumbing, and tiling, but our skilled and intuitive team is handy with all types of construction works. We are committed to delivering high-quality craftsmanship and exceptional service on every project, big or small.

Our mission is to transform your ideas into reality, creating durable and beautiful spaces that stand the test of time. With a focus on innovation and customer satisfaction, DEW Drop Construction is your trusted partner for all your building needs.
